<objective>
Close BLOCKERs CR-02, CR-03, and CR-04 — three correctness defects in the reporting and expiry subsystems:

CR-02: on_fill_event builds fill_payload without strategy_name or side. The daily P&L aggregator reads both fields from fill events; every fill buckets under "_unknown_" and every SELL is sign-flipped (reported as negative P&L). Both fields are available from tp_persisted at the time of writing.

CR-03: send_daily_pnl_digest writes a daily_pnl audit event claiming the digest was delivered even when _send_dm_blocks_respecting_quiet_hours silently suppressed the DM due to quiet hours. The audit log is the project's trustworthy record; this is an integrity defect.

CR-04: The expiry sweep DMs the operator with category=routine_fill, which is suppressed during quiet hours. A real-money proposal expiring during the quiet window produces zero operator signal — a silent dropped-trade-decision, exactly the class of failure the phase brief designates as must-catch.

Purpose: The daily P&L digest must show correct per-strategy P&L and correct sign on SELL fills. The audit log must reflect reality. Trade expiry is an action-needed signal and must use a non-suppressible category.

Output: Three surgical source changes (executor, daily_pnl, expiry) plus targeted regression tests.
</objective>
